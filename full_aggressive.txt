The combination of these factors could result in a substantial loss, potentially exceeding acceptable risk parameters.

Given the significant downside risk, a more cautious approach is warranted. I recommend immediately reducing the position size by at least fifty percent to mitigate potential losses. We must prioritize the preservation of capital and avoid unnecessary exposure to potentially catastrophic events.
