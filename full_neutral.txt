The market rewards calculated risk, and an overly risk-averse approach can be just as detrimental to long-term wealth accumulation as unchecked speculation.

It acknowledges the high conviction in the bullish trend while providing a buffer against significant drawdowns.

Entry Price: Current market price at the time of execution. Given the prevailing bullish trend, entering at market is acceptable to capture immediate momentum.

Stop Loss: 235.00. I will maintain the stop loss at $235.00. This level provides a reasonable buffer below the immediate support of $240 and the more significant support at $220. While the Safe Analyst suggests a tighter stop, a wider stop here allows for normal market fluctuations and prevents premature exit from a potentially strong trend due to minor pullbacks, which is common in an overbought market. The risk of a sharp decline below $235 is mitigated by the reduced position size, ensuring that any loss, while potentially significant in percentage terms of the trade, is contained in absolute dollar terms relative to the total capital. This approach balances the need to allow the trade room to breathe with the imperative to protect principal.
