Apple’s future trajectory hinges on its ability to sustain innovation, expand into new markets (e.g., augmented reality, automotive), and effectively manage its supply chain. The company’s strong brand loyalty and vast ecosystem provide a competitive advantage, but continued success will require adapting to evolving consumer demands and navigating potential regulatory challenges.
