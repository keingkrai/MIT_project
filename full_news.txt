**Apple (AAPL) News Summary (November 23, 2025 - December 2, 2025):**

*   **AI Leadership Changes:**
    *   John Giannandrea, Apple's head of AI, is stepping down and will retire in Spring 2026.
    *   Amar Subramanya, formerly of Microsoft and Google, will replace him as VP of AI. This is a key focus for Apple, as it is seen by some as lagging behind competitors in the AI race.
*   **Intel Partnership:**
    *   Reports indicate a potential deal where Intel will manufacture Apple's low-end M-series chips starting in 2027. This could be a significant boost for Intel's foundry business.
*   **Product and Market Performance:**
    *   Apple shares have hit new all-time intraday and closing highs, with a 14.4% YTD rally, indicating investor optimism.
    *   Strong iPhone 17 sales and market share gains are noted.
    *   Samsung is releasing a new foldable phone that could compete with Apple's expected foldable model.
*   **Legal and Regulatory:**
    *   Apple is facing potential class action lawsuits in the EU over App Store practices.
    *   Apple may resist a directive in India to preload a state-run cyber safety app, due to security concerns.
*   **Market Sentiment:**
    *   Overall sentiment towards AAPL is mixed, with some articles being bullish and others neutral.
    *   Some analysts are projecting continued growth, while others are more cautious.
    *   The AI leadership change is a significant event for Apple, with potential impact on future performance and market position.
*   **Other Tech News:**
    *   The mHealth market is poised for growth driven by smartphone adoption and rising chronic conditions.
    *   OpenAI is developing a large language model called Garlic to counter Google's AI gains.
